About Us

We are a leading online technology store, with more than 10 years of experience. We are experts in computers and electronics and sell hardware components for electronic devices.

Our History

We started as a small local business and expanded to different areas of the country. Today, we have distribution locations worldwide, including A Coruña, Barcelona, Tokyo, Brussels, Lima, Sydney, Alaska, and Toronto.
