About Us

Welcome to 'Jurasic Parts', your trusted online store for everything technology related. We've been here since 2012 and are passionate about making technology available to everyone. We sell a wide range of computer parts, gadgets and electronics, from the basics to the latest innovations.

At Jurassic Parts we believe in simplicity. Our website is easy to navigate and we offer clear and useful information on all our products. We offer competitive prices and fast, reliable shipping, so you can get your technology quickly and without spending a lot of money.

We are more than a store: we are a community of technology enthusiasts. Our customer service team is always ready to answer your questions, and whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting out, Jurassic Parts is here to help you explore the exciting world of technology.

Our History

Our company started as a small neighborhood store. We focused on serving our local customers and building strong relationships within our community. Over time, we grew. We started reaching out to nearby towns and then larger cities. Later we saw the need for our products and services in more places, so we decided to expand.

Today we are proud to say that we have distribution centers all over the world. We have come a long way from our small store. You can find us in many different places. We are in A Coruña, Spain, and also in Barcelona. We have locations in Tokyo, Japan, and Brussels, Belgium. We also have distribution in Lima, Peru, and Sydney, Australia. We even have offices in Alaska (USA) and Toronto (Canada). We have worked hard to bring our products and services to people all over the world. We want to make sure that everyone has access to what we have to offer, wherever they live.
